As Principal Investigator, Prof. Yoon directs RPL's research across Monte Carlo neutron transport, high-fidelity deterministic core solvers, and AI-enhanced reactor simulation. His work carries computational reactor physics from first-principles methods through to industrial PWR and next-generation SMR core design, and underpins the lab's two-step pin-by-pin code system.
